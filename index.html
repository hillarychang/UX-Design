Background

The YouTube app has almost everything you want: it has pages to display trending videos, content from creators you love, and videos filtered by subject. However, one recurring issue I discovered on the app is that creators can spread misinformation or harmful statements on the app and prevent investigation by disabling comments or deleting comments on their videos. Although there are moderators on the app, it is extremely tough for the team to monitor all content within the widely expanding community.  Therefore, to protect the YouTube community, I created a feature that allows users to tag creators and communicate with one another about any issues they see 

Creation

In order to prevent creators from spreading harmful content on YouTube, I decided to implement another page onto the app to enhance users' experiences. YouTube allows creators to communicate with subscribers through the post feature. However, the only way for viewers to communicate with others or the creator is by commenting on a video. The creator can easily delete or disable comments. Therefore, I decided to create a feature that would allow users to communicate with the creator or with other users through posts directed at them or with the option of private messaging.

About Each Page

Page 1

The first page has two options that link to either the discussions page or messages page. Under the new buttons, the page displays posts from online users.

Page 2

This is the discussions page. On this page, users can search for each creator to view all posts surrounding the creator.

Page 3

This is the chat page, which displays all of the messages the user has, and allows the user to communicate with other users privately.
